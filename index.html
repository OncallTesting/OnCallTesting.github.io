Non Invasive Prenatal DNA Test

Non-Invasive Prenatal Paternity (NIPP) testing establishes paternity by analyzing a blood sample from the mother and a DNA sample (like a cheek swab) from the potential father.

This test provides results with over 99.9% confidence and can be conducted as early as 7 weeks into the pregnancy.
